An Insights into Men's Mental Health

Depression consumes your day-to-day life and interferes with your ability to work, study, eat, sleep, and have fun.

Stigma and Barriers

Men often face societal stigma and traditional expectations that discourage them from seeking help for mental health issues. This stigma can be a significant barrier to addressing mental health concerns.

Higher Suicide Rates

Men generally have higher rates of completed suicides than women. This may be attributed to factors such as less help-seeking behavior, method choices, and societal expectations around stoicism.

Prevalence of Substance Abuse

Men may be more likely to turn to substance abuse as a coping mechanism for mental health challenges. Substance abuse can exacerbate existing mental health issues or contribute to the development of new ones.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our FAQ section for comprehensive insights into HeHaven's services, confidentiality measures, appointment scheduling, and more. Find clarity on commonly asked questions as you embark on your journey toward improved mental health.

  • How do I schedule an appointment?

    Booking an appointment is easy! Simply visit our "Make Appointment" section on the website and follow the guided steps. You can also call our helpline for assistance.

  • What types of services do you offer?

    We provide a wide range of mental health services tailored to individual needs, including counseling, therapy, support groups, workshops, and more. Explore our "Our Services" section for detailed information.

  • Are your services confidential?

    Yes, confidentiality is a top priority. Our team adheres to strict privacy standards, ensuring that your personal information remains confidential and secure.

  • Are virtual appointments available?

    Yes, we offer virtual appointments to ensure flexibility and accessibility. You can connect with our therapists from the comfort of your home.

  • What do I do in case of a crisis?

    If you're in crisis, please reach out to our crisis intervention services immediately. Dial our emergency helpline number +91 82582 57825.
